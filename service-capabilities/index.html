Service Capabilities

Within our materials handling operation, experienced production chemists work alongside a team of dedicated warehouse staff to handle hazardous materials in a safe and sustainable way.

Repackaging and Re-labelling Services

Our supply chain activities continue to expand as our reputation for taking on specialist work with hazardous chemicals is increasingly acknowledged. We are skilled and experienced in:

  • Repackaging hazardous chemicals
  • Repackaging damaged or corroded drums
  • Handling air and moisture-sensitive compounds under controlled atmosphere
  • Handling pyrophorics using inert atmosphere glove box environment
  • Providing an outsourced product sampling service

We ensure that all activities are carried out in full-compliance with UK, EU REACH and GHS legislation.

Warehousing and Distribution

Our storage facility is modern and secure and includes a chilled temperature controlled unit. Our experienced team is fully trained in shipping goods according to IATA, IMDG and ADR requirements and we have expert knowledge of carriers specialising in the transport of hazardous goods. We can also call upon the assistance of our in-house Dangerous Goods Safety Advisor (DGSA), to provide a comprehensive solution.

CLP Compliant Labelling and Safety Data Sheets

In Europe, the CLP Regulations (Classification, Labelling and Packaging of Hazardous Substances and Mixtures Regulations) regulate how chemicals should be classified, labelled and packaged.

Using Athena Advantage software from Lycos, our highly-trained and experienced compilers can help ensure that your Safety Data Sheets (SDS) and labels for any chemical products are of a high standard and fully-compliant with CLP and GHS legislation.

We provide:

  • An outsourced service
  • EU-compliant software
  • Substances or mixtures
  • Highly-trained, competent compilers
  • Convert from CHIP to new CLP format
  • Available in 27 languages including Chinese
  • Dangerous goods notes compliant with IATA and IMDG

New Service Developments

We are currently evaluating a number of commercial opportunities related to the storage, repackaging and distribution of finished medicine products. An application to the UK Medicines and Healthcare products Regulatory Agency for a wholesale dealer licence is under review.
